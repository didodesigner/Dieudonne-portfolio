About Me

I’m a passionate graphic designer based in Kigali, Rwanda. With over 5 years of experience, I specialize in branding, social media visuals, and modern design solutions for businesses of all sizes. My goal is to help brands tell their stories through compelling visual communication.

When I'm not designing, you can find me exploring Kigali's vibrant art scene, hiking in the nearby hills, or experimenting with new design trends. I believe in the power of creativity to transform ideas into impactful realities.
